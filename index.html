What is GuruDev?

The word Guru means an advisor or teacher, and Dev is a sanskrit word meaning light. Thus Gurudev turns ignorance into enlightenment. The word GuruDev is an word used to denote master/teacher by students in ancient India.

In my case, I used the word Dev for abbreviation of Developer ;)
